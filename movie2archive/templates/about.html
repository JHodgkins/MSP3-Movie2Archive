What inspired Movie2archive

Movie2archive was inspired through the need to archive a large collection of movies left to my family by my late father. This inspiration has motivated and become a peersonal mission to help others in a similar situation, as this is undoubtedly not a unique case, by creating Movie2archive it will hopefully continue to help the community.

Movie2archive is also there for the movie fans out there that love to collect these incrediable block busters, but after time it can become hard to know exactly where every movie is when stored around the average home.

What can we do next

Movie2archive will continue to build on its foundations to help people, we have a strong desire also to help the environment and encourage recycling through normal means or through friends and family and the community so everyone can enjoy movies no matter your age.
